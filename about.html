Pacific Rim

At Pacific Rim, we strive to provide a casual fine dining experience in a unique and elegant atmosphere. We present the finest selection of specialty dishes, all of various cultures within the Pacific Rim region.

Whether you are celebrating a special occasion or simply looking for a dining experience like none other, we hope that you enjoy the innovative cuisine and surroundings presented through Pacific Rim's unsurpassed commitment to service and quality.

The Asian Fusion Concept

The term "Pacific Rim" is used to describe the vast Geographic region of nations bordering the Pacific Ocean and the island nations within it, including China, Japan, Thailand, Malaysia, Australia, Philippines, Hawaii, California, Mexico, and more. This area encompasses an array of varying ethnic backgrounds and cultures, all of which have influenced our specialty dishes. We have selected various components of traditional recipes from this region to create exquisite combinations. This style of dining can also be referred to as Asian Fusion, which is the result of innovatively blending traditional ingredients to create distinctive tastes.

We are pleased to represent the cultures of this region with a variety of delicious entrees and stir fries, as well as salads, sandwiches, and delectable dessert creations.
